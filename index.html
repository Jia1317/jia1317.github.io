Our Mission

We’re a 100% skateboarder-owned, non-profit brand based in Los Angeles—men and women, united by pavement and purpose.

Every board we build helps clean up waste. Every piece of clothing gets a second life. You skate. We heal the planet.

Curly.Co exists so you can feel good about what you ride—and what you wear.
